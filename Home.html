What is Doge

Dogecoin is a decentralized, peer-to-peer digital currency that enables you to easily send money online. Think of it as "the internet currency."

Next steps in Doge

Dogecoin has been in development since 2013. it was originally created by Billy Markus and Jackson Palmer. With a purpose of creating a more fun crypto currency the joke has now become a reality.
